# IntelliFlow AI Platform - Case Studies

## Case 1: Major Retail Chain - Demand Forecasting System

### Challenges
- High waste due to overstock
- Opportunity loss due to stockouts
- Difficulty in forecasting seasonal fluctuations

### Solution
Implemented IntelliFlow AI Platform to achieve:
- Demand forecasting model integrating weather, events, and historical sales data
- Automatic calculation of optimal order quantities per store
- Visualization via real-time dashboards

### Results
- **Inventory waste reduced by 35%**
- **Stockouts reduced by 50%**
- **Ordering man-hours reduced by 70%**
- ROI: Investment recovered in 6 months

### Client Testimonial
> "We were amazed by the accuracy of the AI demand forecasting. The forecasts for new products and seasonal items improved dramatically, significantly reducing waste."
> — Logistics Director

## Case 2: Manufacturing - Quality Anomaly Detection System

### Challenges
- Delays in detecting defective products on the production line
- Limitations of visual inspection and human error
- Difficulty in securing inspectors

### Solution
- Automatic visual inspection using Image Recognition AI
- Real-time anomaly detection alerts
- Automatic classification of defects and root cause analysis

### Results
- **Inspection accuracy achieved 99.5%**
- **Inspection process automated by 80%**
- **Defective product outflow reduced by 95%**
- Improved production efficiency through early identification of defect causes

### Client Testimonial

### Results

## Case 4: Healthcare - Diagnosis Support System

### Results
- **Diagnostic time reduced by 50%**
- **Oversight rate reduced by 30%**
- **Improved quality of telemedicine**
- Balanced doctor workload reduction with diagnostic accuracy
